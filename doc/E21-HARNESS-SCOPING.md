## The four arms

1. **KB+ME four-layer** — typed routing to knowledge-base (supersession) and memory-engine (bi-temporal), as in the pilot.
2. **Flat single store** — SQLite+FTS5 control, as in the pilot.
3. **Flat + four per-type persistence-policy profiles** — NEW: same flat schema, per-FactType decay/persistence policies. This is the falsification-register control: if it matches arm 1 within CI, the thesis restates as _persistence policies, not architecture_.
4. **Learned router** — NEW: cheap-model classifier replacing the oracle (fit on oracle labels, evaluate on held-out split).

Scale-up: all 10 BEAM categories (currently 2) × 100K split → 400 questions (currently 80).
